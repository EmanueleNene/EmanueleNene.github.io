Chocolate Chip Cookies

The perfect balance of chewy and crispy with melted chocolate chunks.

Ingredients

  • 250g All-purpose flour
  • 150g Brown sugar
  • 100g Granulated sugar
  • 200g Unsalted butter (melted)
  • 1 Egg + 1 Yolk
  • 200g Dark chocolate chunks
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp Sea salt

Instructions

  1. Whisk together the melted butter and sugars until smooth.
  2. Add the egg, yolk, and vanilla; whisk until light and pale.
  3. Fold in the flour and chocolate chunks until just combined.
  4. Chill the dough for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Bake at 180Â°C for 10-12 minutes until edges are golden.

Chef's Note

Use high-quality dark chocolate (60% cocoa or higher) for the best flavor profile. Don't overbake!
