Sourdough Bread

Classic artisanal sourdough with a perfect crust and airy crumb.

Ingredients

  • 500g Strong bread flour
  • 350g Water (70% hydration)
  • 100g Active sourdough starter
  • 10g Sea salt

Instructions

  1. Mix flour and water and let autolyse for 1 hour.
  2. Add starter and salt, mix until fully incorporated.
  3. Perform bulk fermentation with stretch and folds every 30 minutes for 3-4 hours.
  4. Shape the dough and refrigerate overnight in a banneton.
  5. Bake in a preheated Dutch oven at 240Â°C for 20 mins covered, then 20 mins uncovered.

Chef's Note

The secret is in the fermentation time. Watch the dough, not the clock!
