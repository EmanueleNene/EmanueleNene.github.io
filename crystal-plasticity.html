Mechanics of Materials

Crystal Plasticity

Crystal plasticity analysis for bi-crystal Ni-base superalloy by adopting compact specimens with different crystal orientations.

Overview

This research involves the analysis of deformation mechanisms in nickel-base superalloys at the crystalline level. Nickel-base superalloys are critical for high-temperature applications, such as in turbine blades for jet engines and power plants.

Methodology

The study utilizes advanced crystal plasticity modeling and finite element analysis (FEA) to simulate how different crystal orientations affect the mechanical response and fatigue life of bi-crystal specimens.
