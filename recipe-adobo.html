Main Dish

Chicken Adobo-ish

A savory, tangy Filipino-inspired braised chicken with a perfectly reduced sauce.

Ingredients

For ~1.2–1.5 kg chicken (about 8–10 wings + 4–6 drumsticks):

  • 120 ml Soy sauce (½ cup)
  • 120 ml Vinegar (½ cup) - Rice vinegar for milder, or white/distilled for sharper
  • 180 ml Water (¾ cup)
  • 6–8 cloves Garlic, smashed
  • 2 Bay leaves
  • 1 tsp Whole black peppercorns (or ½ tsp ground pepper)
  • 1–2 tbsp Brown sugar or honey (optional, balances acidity)

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a pot with the chicken.
  2. Bring to a simmer over medium-high heat.
  3. Lower heat and simmer, covered, until chicken is cooked through (~25-30 mins).
  4. Remove the lid and increase heat to medium-high.
  5. Reduce the sauce until it thickens and coats the chicken beautifully. Expect to end up with ~200–280 ml of sauce (2–4 tbsp per portion).

Scaling Rule

Keep it simple: Soy : Vinegar : Water = 1 : 1 : 1.5

Example: 100 ml soy, 100 ml vinegar, 150 ml water.
