Truffle Pasta

A creamy, luxurious pasta dish featuring Italian black truffles.

Ingredients

  • 400g Tagliatelle or Pappardelle
  • 50g Black truffle (fresh or preserved)
  • 75g Unsalted butter
  • 60g Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • Fresh black pepper
  • Salt for pasta water

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. While pasta boils, melt butter in a large pan over low heat.
  3. Add a ladle of pasta water to the butter to form an emulsion.
  4. Toss the pasta into the butter sauce, adding parmesan and more pasta water as needed.
  5. Shave fresh truffle over the pasta just before serving.

Chef's Note

Never cook the truffle! The aroma is volatile and will be lost. Thin shavings at the end are key.
